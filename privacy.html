Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

The Short Version

InvoiceForge does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. All invoice processing happens entirely within your browser. Your invoice data, client list, rate sheet, and uploaded logo are stored only in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device.

What We Collect

We collect no personally identifiable information. We do not require account creation or email submission at any point.

How Your Data Is Stored

Invoice data, saved clients, rate sheet entries, and your uploaded logo are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data exists only on your device. Clearing your browser cache or localStorage will remove this data. We have no access to it.

PDF Generation

Invoice PDFs are generated entirely in your browser using the open-source html2canvas and jsPDF libraries. No data is sent to any server during this process.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-focused and does not use cookies or fingerprinting. It collects only aggregated, anonymized traffic data (page views, referrers) and does not track individual users.

Advertising

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads. InvoiceForge does not sell advertising or share user data with advertisers.

Third-Party Libraries

We load fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies to font requests. We load jsPDF and html2canvas from Cloudflare CDN. No user data is transmitted in these requests.

Contact

Questions about this policy: GitHub Issues.
