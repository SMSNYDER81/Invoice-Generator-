About InvoiceForge

InvoiceForge is a free, privacy-first invoice generator built for freelancers, contractors, and small businesses who need a professional invoice fast — without signing up for yet another platform.

Why We Built This

Every major invoice tool on the market uses the free generator as a funnel to capture your email and push you toward a paid subscription. We think that's the wrong model. Most people sending invoices need a clean PDF, not cloud sync and automated reminders they'll never use.

InvoiceForge gives you the tool. Nothing else.

How It Works

  • Everything runs in your browser — no server processes your data
  • Your invoice, client list, rate sheet, and logo are saved to localStorage on your device
  • PDFs are generated client-side using html2canvas and jsPDF
  • No account, no email, no credit card — ever

How We Stay Free

InvoiceForge is supported by non-intrusive display advertising. We do not sell user data because we don't collect any. We do not have a premium tier. The tool is and will remain free.
